How do i place an order?

Easy steps to place an order.

  1. Select the product by clicking on it..
  2. Fill the required fields and then click Add to Cart.
  3. Click on Place order option and than click continue.
  4. Fill address details and click submit below.
  5. It will show amount to pay than click submit. which will lead to payment page.
  6. Payment page of Payumoney will open which will gove various options to pay like phonepe,paytm googlepay cards and netbanking etc.
